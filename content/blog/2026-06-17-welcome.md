## Why a blog
  
In too many ways it is a natural experience to wait until things are highly polished and presentable before allowing others to see them. Many thoughts and ideas are lost simply because we have hesitated sharing them until we have decided they are worth sharing.  
  
  
This blog is an attempt to break this habit, present ideas, ramblings, and musings I find interesting, regardless if I feel they are "correct" or "polished".  
  
  
## Connecting more with our shared human experience
In a world that is pushing us towards isolation and vain ideas of perfection this blog will be an effort to connect again with curiosity and the beauty of conscious thought and imperfection.
